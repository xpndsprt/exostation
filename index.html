Welcome aboard, Commander

You're founding a deep-space station and a crew of wildly different alien species — and racing to light the Sector Beacon.

🎯 Your goal — the Sector Beacon

Grow your crew, bank credits, and host a diverse roster — then build each species' signature module and keep it staffed to charge the Beacon. Bring all five online to win.

🫁 Air is zoned — seal it

Species breathe different gases (O₂, CH₄, Cl₂, NH₃, H₂). Each needs its own sealed wing with the matching generator, linked by doors (they pass crew but hold gas). The wrong air — or two gases mixing — is lethal. H₂ + O₂ in one room explodes; Cl₂ corrodes machinery; some crews want their wing hot or cold.

🤝 The political web — who you house together matters

Every species loves or hates others. Humans & Korro HATE each other; Thol & Vry'l LOVE; the Drenn & Naaz get on with everyone. Friends sharing a room boost mood and production; rivals turn it tense — and a tense room eventually erupts into a fight. Group allies, wall off rivals.

A Getting Started checklist sits in the corner: seal a room → power it → add air, food, a bunk and a dock, and your first crew will fly in.
