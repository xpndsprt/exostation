EXOSTATION — HOW TO WIN

The whole game on a page. Follow the arrows.

① The win condition — the Sector Beacon

To win you must keep every beacon species happy & housed at once — so the whole game is really "host all five, all comfortable, all powered."

The cast — species & their beacon devices

Ten species pass through your station. Five are "beacon" species — each unlocks one signature module (2×2, ¢800) that charges the Sector Beacon while that species lives, powered and in its own room. The other five are guests & specialist crew with no beacon — host them for income, peace, or muscle.

② The survival chain — never break a link

Read left→right. Break an earlier link and everything after it fails. If overwhelmed: pause (Space), fix Air then Power, unpause.

③ Fix-it priority (top first)

Tension is the symptom — prevent it upstream (good food + don't force rivals together), don't fight it.

④ Gas zoning — wall species into their air

O₂ — Human · Drenn · Vry'l · Korro · Sszra CH₄ — Thol · Vorn Cl₂ — Chlorithe (corrodes!) NH₃ — Naaz (keep cold) H₂ — Voltaar (keep hot)
Each non-O₂ species needs a sealed wing with its own generator (and a door airlock). Keep H₂ far from O₂.

⑤ Food loop (crew haul it)

Producers buffer output; crew carry it to Storage, then to the Synth & Tables. More crew + Storage Floor = smoother food.

⑥ Money loop

Mining → Trade Hub → credits → bigger station & research. Also: lodging (Hotels) & fuel sales add income.

⑦ The road to victory

Each phase funds the next. The end-game is purely social: every beacon species comfortable in its own air, all five modules lit at once.
